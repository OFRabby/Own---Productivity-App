# Own - Productivity App 🚀

A simple yet powerful productivity application designed for seamless task management and smart shop calculations. Built with Flutter, this app helps you stay organized and manage your daily expenses in one place.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📝 Task Management
- **Full Control:** Create, edit, and delete tasks effortlessly.
- **Categorization:** Organize your life with built-in categories:
  - 🎓 University
  - 💍 Wife
  - 👤 Personal
  - ✈️ Tour
  - 🤝 Promises
- **Visual Tasks:** Attach photos to your tasks using your camera or gallery.
- **Quick Search:** Find any task instantly with the search functionality.

### 🔔 Smart Notifications
- **Scheduled Reminders:** Get notified exactly when your tasks are due.
- **Status Alerts:** Receive confirmation notifications upon task completion.
- **Reliable Performance:** Works perfectly in the background with persistent foreground services.
- **Interactive:** Tap lock screen notifications to jump straight into the app.

### 🧮 Shop Calculator
- **Purchase List Mode:** Calculate total costs (Price × Quantity) for your shopping trips.
- **Max Quantity Calculator:** Find out exactly how many items you can buy within your budget.

### 🎨 UI/UX & Experience
- **Material Design 3:** Modern, clean, and intuitive user interface.
- **Adaptive Themes:** Full support for Dark and Light modes (follows system settings).
- **Smart Permissions:** User-friendly dialogs for notification and camera access.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Framework:** [Flutter](https://flutter.dev/)
- **State Management:** [Riverpod](https://riverpod.dev/)
- **Database:** SQLite (via `sqflite`)
- **Notifications:** `flutter_local_notifications`
- **Background Tasks:** `workmanager`
- **Image Handling:** `image_picker`
